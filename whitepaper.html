Tokenomics:

BMCat operates on the Ethereum blockchain, utilizing ERC-20 standards for compatibility and interoperability. The total supply of BMCat is capped at 1 billion tokens, ensuring scarcity and value appreciation over time. A portion of the tokens is allocated for the lottery prize pool, while the rest is distributed through liquidity provision, community incentives, and development efforts.

Lottery Game Mechanics:

The core feature of Billion MC Cat is its integrated lottery game, which offers users the opportunity to participate in regular draws for a chance to win significant prizes.

  • Participation1: Users can purchase BMCat tokens and hold them in their wallets to automatically qualify for lottery entry. Each BMCat token held corresponds to a certain number of entries into the lottery pool, increasing the chances of winning.
  • Participation2: Users can purchase Lottery tickets.
  • Draw Schedule: Lottery draws are held on a regular basis, with the frequency and timing determined by the community through governance mechanisms. This ensures transparency and fairness in the selection process.
  • Prize Distribution: The prize pool consists of a percentage of transaction fees generated within the BMCat ecosystem. Winners are selected randomly from eligible participants, and prizes are distributed automatically to their wallets.
  • Community Governance: Billion MC Cat operates as a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), allowing token holders to participate in decision-making processes through voting mechanisms. This ensures that the community has a say in the development and governance of the platform.
